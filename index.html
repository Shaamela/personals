    WHAT IS TAJWEED?

    Tajweed is the science of pronouncing the words of the Quran correctly, using the rules of Arabic grammar and phonetics. It is an essential part of Quran recitation, as it ensures that the words are pronounced accurately and beautifully.

    WHY LEARN QURAN WITH TAJWEED

    Learning Quran with Tajweed is crucial for several reasons:

    • Accurate Recitation: Tajweed helps you pronounce the word of the Quran correctly, ensuring that you recite the Quran as it was revealed.
    • Improved Understanding: By learning Tajweed you will be able to understand the meaning of the Quran more accurately, as the pronunciation of the words affects the meaning
    • Spiritual Growth: Reciting the Quran with Tajweed brings a sense of peace and tranquillity and helps you connect with the words of Allah.
